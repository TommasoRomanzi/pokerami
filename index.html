What is PokerAmi?

Play poker


How can I use it?

1. Choose a trusted person in the group. He will be the dealer, the person that deal the game
2. The dealer will create a code and give it to the group. A max of 8 people can join.
3. Once everybody is ready everybody choose the amount of money they have on their plate.
4. The dealer will start the match and the roundes. During the match every player can Bet/Raise/Fold and at the end of the game the dealer will assign the prize to the winner.
5. Everybody can cashout and at the end of the game the software will tell you how much you owe/earned.
